How companies can rip you off on

hair loss treatments

There are only 3 scientifically proven ways to slow down or reverse male balding

LEARN MORE

Finasteride (Propecia)

By the far the most effective treatment we have today - Propecia is a drug that directly stops your body producing DHT (Dihydrotestosterone) - the stuff that is responsible for your hair loss. By directly reducing the amount of this in your body - propecia gets to the root (no pun intended) of the problem and can really slow down and even reverse hair loss.





Minoxidil - (Rogaine)

A liquid or foam applied to the scalp, minoxidil differs from propecia in that it doesn’t directly address the cause of hair loss. Instead, minoxidil is believed to widen the blood vessels that reach the hair follicle (vasodilation) - allowing more vital nutrients to reach the hair, and thus slowing down the problem. While many people experience great results with Minoxidil - because it doesn’t directly target the cause of hair loss - its results are limited when compared with Finasteride.

Ketocanazole (Nizoral)

A shampoo that has been shown to have moderate effects on hair loss - it works by ensuring the scalp is healthy and free of sebum (an oily substance that builds up on the scalp and can damage hair growth).

These three are the only drugs that are scientifically proven to safely slow down or reverse your hair loss.


Any company that offers other treatments or equipment is wasting your time and money.

Moreover, a year’s supply of all the above medications need not cost more than a couple of hundred dollars. 

Many companies offer ‘hair programs’ and clinics that sell you combinations of these products, often along with other products that have little to no scientific credibility, for thousands of dollars a year. Save yourself money, time and hassle and don’t be conned into buying anything you don’t need.
